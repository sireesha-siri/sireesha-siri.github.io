Every common man who by his sheer grit and hard work achieve sucess should share his story with the rest for they may find inspiration and strength to go on, in his story. The 'Wings of Fire' is one such autobiography by visionary scientist Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam, who knows from Every humble begininings rose to be the President of India. The book is full of insights, personal moments and life experience of Dr.Kalam. It gives us an understanding on his journey of sucess.
